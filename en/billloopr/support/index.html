Common help topics

  • Create clients and projects before recording billable time.
  • Use the timer to record work sessions and review them before invoicing.
  • Use invoice records to prepare, preview, share, and manage billing documents.
  • Use in-app controls to delete clients, projects, invoices, time entries, or related records.

Data and privacy

BillLoopr works without an account. Records stay locally and may sync privately through your Apple Account when iCloud is available. Exports happen only when you choose them.
