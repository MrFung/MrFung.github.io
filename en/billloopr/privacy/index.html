BillLoopr

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 11, 2026

BillLoopr is a billable time, project record, and invoice preparation app developed by MrFung. This policy explains what information the app handles.

Information stored in the app

BillLoopr works without an account. It may store clients, projects, time entries, invoice and income records, notes, currencies, rates, preferences, and related settings that you enter.

Local storage

App data is stored locally using Apple platform technologies. When iCloud is available, supported data may also sync to your private iCloud database. BillLoopr does not operate its own sync server.

Private iCloud sync

BillLoopr uses Apple CloudKit to sync supported data across your devices in a private database associated with your Apple Account. An available account status does not confirm every change has finished syncing.

No tracking or advertising

BillLoopr has no third-party ads, no third-party analytics SDK, and does not track you across apps or websites.

Sharing and exports

When you export, share, copy, or send records, selected information may leave the app through a destination you choose, such as Mail, Files, Messages, or another app.

Support requests

If you email support, we may receive your email address and the information you choose to provide, used only to answer the request.

Payments and financial information

BillLoopr helps prepare time and invoice records. It does not process client payments or provide banking, tax, legal, or accounting advice.

Deleting data

You can delete supported business records inside the app. With iCloud sync, in-app deletions sync to other devices. Deleting the app from one device may leave data in iCloud or on other devices.

Children

BillLoopr is not directed to children and is intended for independent professionals and business use.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the app changes. The effective date will change when material updates are made.

Contact
