Common help topics

  • Create a workplace, people, and shift types before entering your shifts or full staff rosters.
  • Use the Me view or person filters to quickly review your shifts and coworkers.
  • After importing a roster image, review recognized results before saving them as shift records.
  • Allow notifications to receive reminders before your shifts.
  • Use in-app delete controls to remove workplaces, people, shifts, reminder rules, or related data when available.

数据与隐私 / Data and privacy

RosterSlate 不需要账号即可使用。除非你主动导出或分享，排班记录主要保存在你的设备本地。

RosterSlate is designed to work without an account. Roster records are stored locally on your device unless you choose to export or share them.

隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

非医疗或工资服务 / Not medical or payroll services

RosterSlate 是通用排班工具，不提供医疗诊断、临床决策、工资发放、税务、法律或银行服务。

RosterSlate is a general roster planning tool. It does not provide medical diagnosis, clinical decisions, payroll, tax, legal, or banking services.
