数据与隐私 / Data and privacy

BillLoopr 无需注册 BillLoopr 账号即可使用。App 记录保存在设备本地；iCloud 可用时，也可能通过你的 Apple 账号私密同步到其它设备。导出和分享仅由你主动触发。

BillLoopr works without creating a BillLoopr account. App records are stored locally and, when iCloud is available, may sync privately across devices through your Apple Account. Exports and sharing happen only when you choose them.

BillLoopr helps organize time, project, income, and invoice records. It does not provide tax, legal, accounting, banking, or payment processing services.
