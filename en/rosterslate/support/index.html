Common help topics

  • Create a workplace, people, and shift types before entering shifts or full rosters.
  • Use the Me view or person filters to review your shifts and coworkers.
  • Review recognized results before saving an imported roster image.
  • Allow notifications to receive reminders before your shifts.
  • Use in-app controls to delete workplaces, people, shifts, reminder rules, or related data.

Data and privacy

RosterSlate works without an account. Roster records stay on your device unless you choose private iCloud sync, export, or sharing.

Privacy Policy

Not medical or payroll services

RosterSlate is a general roster tool. It does not provide diagnosis, clinical decisions, payroll, tax, legal, or banking services.
