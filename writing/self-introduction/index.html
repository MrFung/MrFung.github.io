全栈开发者，AICoding 实践者，独立产品打磨者，也是在越野跑、马拉松和划桨板里练习长期主义的人。

Full-stack builder, AICoding practitioner, independent product maker, and someone who practices long-term thinking through trail running, marathons, and paddleboarding.

中文

把真实工作流做成能长期维护的工具。

我关注全栈开发、AICoding 和独立产品。相比只做一个漂亮的展示，我更在意工具能不能真正进入日常工作流，能不能减少混乱，能不能长期被维护。

运动给了我另一套相似的判断方式。ITRA 430、马拉松大众二级、红十字救护员、越野跑和划桨板，这些标签背后都是同一件事：稳定训练、清晰节奏、尊重风险，然后持续往前。

English

Building tools that can stay useful.

I focus on full-stack development, AICoding, and independent products. Instead of making one-off demos, I care about tools that can become part of real workflows, reduce friction, and stay maintainable over time.

Endurance sports shape the same way of thinking. ITRA 430, marathon amateur level 2, Red Cross first aider, trail running, and paddleboarding all point to the same habit: train steadily, keep a clear rhythm, respect risk, and keep moving.
